Digests

Spin 2023 Digests

All invited speakers and poster presenters must submit a two page digest for inclusion in the conference Digest Book. We also encourage authors to upload their digests to IEEE Xplore. Each published digest will receive its own digital object identifier (DOI) and be citable. Instructions on how to do this will be sent to authors later in the year.


This year the digest format has been changed to a two-column format. Please use one of the templates below to prepare your digest.


Microsoft Word - Digest Word template

Latex - Digest Latex template


Invited speakers should send their digests to the Program Co-Chairs, while poster presenters should send their digests to the Poster Co-Chairs. Please visit the Conference Committee web page to find their contact details.


If you have any questions about the digest format, or digest submission please contact 464276130@qq.com.
