Presentation Information

Poster Presentations

  • Posters should be printed to fit the onsite presentation board dimensions of 101 cm (40 inches) wide by 152 cm (60 inches) high. The poster should include title, authors and their affiliations.
  • The poster should be also submitted in a pdf format and a recorded presentation video (5-10 minutes) by August 24th for being available in the post-conference online archive.

Invited Presentations

  • Invited speakers please submit a pre-recorded presentation video (25 minutes) by August 24th.
  • As a reminder we ask all invited speakers to submit a poster for their talk as well for display and discussion during the networking sessions.

Posters should be printed to fit the onsite presentation board dimensions of 101 cm (40 inches) wide by 152 cm (60 inches) high. The poster should include title, authors and their affiliations.
