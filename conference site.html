For PhD Candidates

PhD Scholarship Information

PhD scholarship available in both Department of Chemistry and Department of Physics:

  • (1) NGS scholarship (interdisciplinary): Monthly stipend of S$3,200, S$4000/year for conference, notebook etc support; (http://www.nus.edu.sg/ngs/NGSS.html)
  • (2) NUS PhD scholarship in Department of Chemistry: S$2000/month for Master and S$2500/month for PhD;
  • (3) NUS PhD scholarship in Department of Physics: S$2000/month for Master and S$2500/month for PhD.

General Requirements

  • (1) At least 4-year B.Sc or B.Sc. (Hons) Second Upper Degree or equivalent
  • (2) GRE Score of Verbal >500, Quantitative >700, and Analytical > 3.5
  • (3) TOFEL Score of >580 (PBT)/260 (CBT)/85 (IBT) or IELTS Score > 6
