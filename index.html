SPIN Thailand 2023

September 25-26, 2023

The third International Workshop on Spintronics will be held in Chiang Mai, Thailand. This in-person workshop follows similar workshops held in 2019 in Ollantaytambo, Peru and in 2022 in San Carlos de Bariloche, Argentina. This workshop is organized by both the program committee and the local committee (to be announced).

Spintronics, also known as spin electronics, is a broad research direction that involves the intrinsic spin of electrons and their associated magnetic moments. Since the 1980s, spintronics has been considered a promising technology for the development of storage devices. The most well-known spintronic application is the high-density hard drive based on the giant magneto-resistance effect, which won the Nobel Prize in Physics in 2007. Recent exciting research progress in the field of spintronics has inspired the development of additional applications, such as magnetic random-access memory (MRAM), racetrack memory, magnetic sensors, spin logic devices, high-frequency devices, and more.

This workshop will cover a wide range of topics from the latest theories, experiments, and applications in the field of spintronics, including MRAM, spin-orbit torques, anti-ferromagnetism, spin waves, spin textures, and novel magnetic materials. We would be delighted if you could come to Chiang Mai to present your recent interesting work in a relaxed atmosphere. The workshop will also include tours of Chiang Mai and the surrounding areas with beautiful scenery.
